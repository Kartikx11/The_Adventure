Discover Pokhara

Pokhara, often referred to as the "Gateway to the Himalayas," is a picturesque city in Nepal known for its stunning natural beauty and tranquil atmosphere. Surrounded by snow-capped mountains, serene lakes, and lush greenery, Pokhara is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.

Must-Visit Attractions:

  • Phewa Lake: Take a boat ride on the tranquil waters of this iconic lake and enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Sarangkot: Wake up early to catch the sunrise from this hilltop vantage point, offering panoramic views of the Annapurna range.
  • World Peace Pagoda: Visit this beautiful pagoda situated atop a hill for stunning views of Pokhara Valley and the Himalayas.
  • Devi's Fall: Explore this mesmerizing waterfall, also known as Patale Chhango, which cascades into an underground cavern.
  • Bindhya Basini Temple: Pay homage to the goddess Bhagwati at this ancient Hindu temple located in the heart of Pokhara.

Experience the charm of Pokhara as you wander through its quaint streets, visit traditional markets, and interact with friendly locals.

Whether you're embarking on a trekking adventure in the Annapurna region, paragliding over the Pokhara Valley, or simply relaxing by the lakeside, Pokhara offers endless opportunities for exploration and relaxation.

Plan your getaway to Pokhara today and immerse yourself in the natural beauty and tranquility of this Himalayan gem!
